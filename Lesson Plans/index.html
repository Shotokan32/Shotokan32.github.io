Ladder Varient 1

In this drill the Karateka starts with their feet facing parallel to the Ladder. They step into the ladder with their nearest foot and then step in with the other foot. Followed by stepping out to the opposite side of the ladder with the first foot and then bring the second foot out. This is then repeated down the Ladder at a fast pace.

-Those who struggle with the cordination of the exercise should focus on which foot they are using to step into and out of the ladder.

-The next level of improvement will be focusing on not taking extra steps and focusing on the minimum amount of steps needed to complete the drill.

-The final improvements should be aimed at speed of execution and how throw the use of their feet and control of the rest of the body, can increase the rate of a single ladder run.
